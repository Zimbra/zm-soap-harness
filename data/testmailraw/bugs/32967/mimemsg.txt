=20

=20

As previously announced, the UKL datacenter UPS maintenance is scheduled
for THIS FRIDAY, 07-Nov at 11:00 PM GMT and this maintenance is expected
to have a minimum 9 hour downtime (actual down time may be longer
depending on how long it takes to bring Properties back online).  This
message contains information for the check-in meeting on Friday and
contact info for the day of the maintenance.

=20

=20

Maintenance Details:

We will be working with Level 3 to perform a power redistribution aimed
at mitigating likely unplanned power outages that could be attributed to
UPS overload.  This power redistribution will include a 9 hour downtime
and Property POCs will need to be online to shut machines down prior to
the maintenance and to bring machines back online after the maintenance.
This work is being performed under approved CMR#151171 -=20
http://cm.ops.yahoo.com/requests.php?action=3Dviewrequest&cmr_id=3D151171=


=20

Check-in Meeting Friday:

There is a meeting invite attached to this message for Friday, 7-Nov.
It is MANDATORY for all teams impacted by the maintenance to attend this
meeting or send a delegate.  If a Property is not represented in this
last check-in call, it will be assumed that your Property is ready for
the maintenance and has no questions prior to the maintenance (no show =
=3D
default acceptance and no questions prior to maintenance).

=20

Contact Info During the Maintenance:

There will be an IRC and voice bridge opened by the OC for the duration
of this event:=20

IRC channel: #ukl-maintenance will be open

Voice bridge passcode: (888) 371-8922 (inside U.S.), or (617) 224-4792
(outside U.S.), participant code: 96628

=20

Twiki review:=20

Thanks to all Properties that have been proactively working to ensure
this maintenance event goes as planned.  As mentioned in previous
communications, we need to have a POC from each Property identified to
bring down machines prior to the maintenance and to bring the machines
back up after the maintenance has been completed.  At this time, we have
identified all POCs, and have worked to identify 98% of orphaned hosts.
Please take a moment to review the UKL UPS Maintenance twiki:=20
http://twiki.corp.yahoo.com/view/EUProdOps/UKLUPSMaintenance2008 and
update the information related to your Property including POC, BCP
failover plan and impacted host verification.  Report any discrepancies
to Jennifer Aina
<http://backyard.yahoo.com/tools/g/employee/profile?user_id=3Dainaj> .

=20

Communication Team:

Because a large number of Mail users who access their email from the UKL
datacenter will not be able to access some or all of their email during
this event, we formed a UKL Maintenance Communication Team.  This team
has been meeting over the past several weeks to ensure that we have
blogs, help pages, Customer Care scripts and templates ready for
endusers who have questions regarding this maintenance.  Part of this
preparation included creating and sending an email notification to
impacted users  - this message was sent to the inboxes of all impacted
users today in order to provide advanced notification to Mail users.  If
you have any concerns regarding enduser communication or support for
this event, please send email to:  UKL-Maint-Comm-Team@example.com.

=20

please contact Jennifer Aina
